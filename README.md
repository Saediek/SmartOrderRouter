##SmartOrderRouter
In a world of dexes,discrepancies is a must take advantage of discrepancies and execute your trade with the best conditions.
Due to different pricing mechanisms among dexes,dicrepancies often exists ,SOR allows you to execute your trade with the best condition in a network,conditions that could
affect the outcome of a trade,SOR factors out the best conditions for a trade while it takes into considerations factors like (slippage,gas-fees,Dexes-fees and price of an asset).
