## Abstract

Smart Order Router (SOR) is a decentralized protocol designed to automate the execution of orders, focusing on obtaining the most favorable price across various decentralized exchanges. The SOR leverages predefined rules, configurations, and algorithms to address the issue of fragmented liquidity. By thoroughly analyzing different offers, the SOR strategically places orders to ensure the best available option is utilized.

In the realm of web3 protocols, numerous factors come into play when selecting the optimal trade. These include considerations such as gas costs for executing trades, output amounts received from the trade, and the time taken for execution. SOR simplifies this entire process for users by factoring in each element to derive the most advantageous outcome.

The SOR's innovative approach aims to streamline the trading process within the decentralized ecosystem, providing users with a seamless experience and maximizing their returns. Through its robust and intelligent order routing mechanism, SOR contributes to enhancing liquidity efficiency and optimizing trade execution for participants in the decentralized marketplace.
